Why stakeholders bet on Prince

I adapt my role to fit the specific needs of each venture. At YourHelpa, I operate as the full-stack engine, handling everything from the technology to the business model and marketing. Conversely, at Prawnbox, I partner with a CTO to focus exclusively on Product and Marketing Strategy for scale, while at Avantaland, I teach these exact sales skills to others, filling the critical gaps that often slow startups down.

Speed of Execution

Instead of waiting for a marketing team to catch up with development, I write the copy and build the campaigns myself. For YourHelpa, this meant I could launch the platform and the advertising simultaneously, effectively cutting our time-to-market in half by removing the friction between product and promotion.

Capital Efficiency

I focus on bootstrapping products to profitability by building organic growth engines early on, rather than relying strictly on paid ads. With Prawnbox, we use a founder-led GTM strategy to acquire app of our delivery platform, which significantly reduced our burn rate and eliminated the immediate need for dilutive venture capital.

Product-Market Fit

I validate every business idea using data-driven SEO and direct-response testing before writing a single line of code. For Avantaland, I tested the core offer using Video Sales Letters (VSLs) first, ensuring there was a hungry market ready to buy before I invested time in building the full curriculum.
